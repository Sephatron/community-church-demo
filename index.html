The Community Church · Honiton

Lives changed by Jesus in East Devon.

An ordinary community of people finding a bigger story. We meet at The Beehive, Honiton, every Sunday at 10:30am — and you're warmly invited.

Plan your first visit What happens on a Sunday

Welcome

However you arrive, there's a place for you here.

Whether you've followed Jesus for decades, wandered away years ago, or you're simply curious — you'll find a warm welcome, honest conversation and zero pressure. Come exactly as you are.

When
