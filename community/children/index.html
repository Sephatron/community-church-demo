Community

Children matter.

Your children are important to us, and we take their care and protection very seriously. They're a valued part of our church family.

All our children's and youth workers are DBS-checked, well known within the church community, and vetted by church leadership — as well as being great fun and gifted at working with kids. We believe God can speak to us whatever our age.

Ages 0–4

Creche

A safe, happy space for babies, toddlers and preschoolers — free play, songs and Bible stories.

Ages 5–11

Activ8

Our children’s group meets during most of the Sunday meeting to worship, learn about God, make friends and have lots of fun.

Bring the whole family.

Groups run every Sunday during our 10:30am gathering. We'd love to meet your little ones.
