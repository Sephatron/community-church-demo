Giving

Generosity changes things.

Everything we do is made possible by the generosity of ordinary people, given first and foremost in response to God's love for us.

At The Community Church we all contribute to the life of our church in many ways — first and foremost in response to God's love for us. We encourage everyone who calls this their home church to give prayerfully, regularly, joyfully and generously, in proportion to their income.
